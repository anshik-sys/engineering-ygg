Our core values.

At the heart of everything we do are values that guide not just what we create, but how we show up—for our users, our team, and the world. We believe in building a platform and products that make it easier for people to express themselves, connect deeply, and share joy through the power of giving.

We're passionate about making gifting more thoughtful, personal, and effortless—turning everyday moments into lasting memories. Our work is rooted in empathy, ensuring we understand and honor the emotions behind every gift, recognising that even the smallest gestures can have the biggest impact.

Engagement

Employee engagement activities.

Our employee engagement initiatives are thoughtfully designed to deepen connections, foster collaboration, and encourage personal growth. By strengthening the bonds between team members, we create a workplace where everyone feels valued, motivated, and inspired to bring their best selves. When these connections flourish, our collective effectiveness and success follow.

Care

Employee benefits.

Dedicated to creating an environment where people feel supported, inspired, and equipped to do their best work. Our benefits go beyond the basics–with comprehensive medical coverage, generous parental leave, high-quality equipments, and a flexible workspace that encourages both focus and creativity.

At the core is a culture of openness and trust, where transparent communication and honest feedback are embraced. Every voice matters, and every team member is genuinely valued, because we believe when people are cared for, they don’t just work, they thrive.

Empowerment

Female engagement.

Passionate about building a truly inclusive workplace, we are committed to supporting and empowering women at every stage of their journey. From flexible working hours and FOP (First Day of Period) leave to maternity leave and other gender-responsive policies, our initiatives are designed to meet real needs with empathy and care.

We strive to create a culture where women feel seen, heard, and supported.
